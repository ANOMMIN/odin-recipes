Ice Cream

Description

Come summer time, many people enjoy ice cream, but how many people actually know how to make it?

  • 2 cups of heavy whipping cream
  • 2 cups half-and-half cream
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  1. Mix all of the igredients together, stirring to dissolve ingredients completely.
  2. Fill the ice cream maker with the mix, and freeze
  3. Serve immediately, or store in a covered container
